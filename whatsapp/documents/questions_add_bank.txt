What is an insured deposit according to the "Deposit insurance Law of Azerbaijan Republic"?
An insured deposit is a part or the whole deposit eligible for compensation in case of an insurance incident at a participating bank, based on the clauses of the "Deposit insurance Law of Azerbaijan Republic."
How are compensations for insured deposits handled in different currencies?
For deposits nominated in national currency, the compensation is paid in AZN; for deposits in multiple currencies, the compensation is paid in AZN as one deposit; for USD deposits, compensation is paid in USD and EUR; and for other currencies, compensation is paid in USD or EUR based on the Central Bank's official exchange rate.
